GParted partition editor

GParted is a free partition editor for graphically managing
your disk partitions.

With GParted you can resize, copy, and move partitions without
data loss, enabling you to:
o Grow or shrink your C: drive
o Create space for new operating systems
o Attempt data rescue from lost partitions
--http://gparted.org/

Rename USB drive
================
I used gparted as described in
  https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RenameUSBDrive.

1. sudo apt-get install gparted.
2. open gparted.
3. choose the thumbdrive from the dropdown in the top-right corner.
4. unmount the volume (right-click on drive)
5. right click and choose "label"
--ubuntu.com
