On UAA ITS debian boothite workstation have F1 to F4 mapped as shortcuts
to gnome workspaces 1,2,3, and 4.  

Mapping in gnome under System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts

In Window Management section for each Action click "Disabled"
under Shortcut until "New shortcut..." appears than click 
appropriate F1-F4 function key to assign.

Action                    Shortcut
----------------------------------
Switch to workspace 1     F1
Switch to workspace 2     F2
Switch to workspace 3     F3
Switch to workspace 4     F4

Click Close

Sweet.
